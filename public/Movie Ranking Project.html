My top 3 Favorite Movies

The Pursuit of Happiness

This movie has motivated me a lot because the story runs around the struggle of Chris Gardner and how he worked hard to feed his family.

This movie featured Will Smith & his real son Jaden Smith.

3 Idiots

It's an amazing Indian movie featuring Aamir Khan, R.Madhvan, Sharman Joshi, Boman Irani, teaches us to follow your passion irrespective of everyone's opinions and do what you love and live happily in the present moment and don't worry too much about the future.

Gravity

It's a science-fiction movie based on surviving in space featuring Sandra Bullock & George clooney.

This is the best space movie to watch which really had gave me goosebumps...
